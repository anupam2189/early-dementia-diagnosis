About



The Early Dementia Diagnosis App is a tool for doctors and carers that enables the condition of dementia patients to be monitored from home.

Currently, a dementia patient's condition is only able to be professionally assessed during infrequent visits to medical clinics, which limits the amount of data that may be gathered, in turn limiting the amount of professional help a doctor can perscribe.

Using the Early Dementia Diagnosis App to frequently assess and update the doctor on a patient's condition away from a clinic increases a patient's chances of receiving the best possible treatment.

Functionality



The Early Dementia Diagnosis App is a web and mobile application that functions on both Android an iOS devices.

As different forms of dementia can be assessed in different ways, the app provides doctors with the ability to create custom tests for their patients.

The building blocks for these tests featured in the app include a speech to text converter, a camera feature, and multiple choice questionnaires.

Use Cases



Doctors

Doctors can utilise the app to create and prescribe tests that allow them to track the progress of their patients.

Carers

Carers who are responsible for the care of patients administer the tests and upload the results for doctors to access.

Patients

Patients diagnosed with dementia undertake the tests to assess the state of their condition.
